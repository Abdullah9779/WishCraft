Privacy-First by Design

WishCraft follows a privacy-first approach: we collect only the information necessary to operate the service, protect it with secure practices, and never sell your personal data to third parties.

Information We Collect

Template Data: When you create a card we temporarily store data such as names, dates, messages, uploaded images, and optional music links to render and share your template.

Contact Information: If you contact support we collect your name and email to respond and resolve requests.

Technical Data: We may log basic technical information (IP address, browser, device) to maintain security and improve performance.

No Account Required: We don‚Äôt require accounts ‚Äî we avoid collecting unnecessary profile data like usernames or passwords unless you explicitly opt in to a feature that needs it.

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery: To render and store your templates and enable sharing.

Support: To reply to contact requests and troubleshoot issues.

Product Improvement: To analyze anonymous usage data and improve features and performance.

Security & Compliance: To protect the service from abuse and comply with legal obligations when required.

Data Storage & Security

Session Storage: Template data is stored in secure sessions to enable editing and sharing.

File Storage: Uploaded images are kept with unique identifiers on secure servers.

Encryption: Data in transit is protected by SSL/TLS and industry-standard security practices are applied to server storage.

Access Control: Templates are accessible via unique links or tokens to prevent unauthorized access.

Backups: We maintain backups to guard against accidental data loss.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

No Sale of Data: We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties.

Service Providers: We may share limited data with trusted vendors (hosting, email services) strictly to operate the service under contractual safeguards.

Legal Requests: We may disclose data when required by law or to protect legal rights.

Consent: We will ask for your explicit consent before any data sharing beyond service operation.

Your Rights & Controls

Access: Access your templates via "My Templates" when available.

Deletion: Delete templates anytime using the delete option.

Download: You may download your created cards for safekeeping.

Correction: Edit or recreate templates to correct content.

Contact: For privacy questions, please contact us via the contact form.

Cookies & Tracking

Essential Cookies: We use cookies required for sessions and template persistence.

No Behavioral Tracking: We do not deploy third-party behavioral trackers that sell data.

Third-Party Content: Embedded content (e.g., music players) may set their own cookies; check those providers' policies.

Browser Controls: You can manage or block cookies via your browser settings.

Children's Privacy

Age: WishCraft is family-friendly. Parents should supervise children using the service.

Parental Consent: Parents control the information children share. We avoid collecting unnecessary data from minors.

Minimal Data: We only collect limited data required to create and share templates.

Safe Environment: We strive to keep the platform safe and appropriate for all ages.

Questions About Privacy?

We're here to help! If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, don't hesitate to reach out.
